# User Guide - DataXplore 2.0 Dashboard

Welcome to the DataXplore 2.0 Dashboard for Company X. This guide is designed to help new users navigate the interface, apply filters effectively, and gain insights from the visualizations.

## 1. Introduction
This dashboard analyzes customer behavior, revenue performance, and experience quality for Company X, a live entertainment venue. It processes data from January to September 2025, covering multiple countries and seating tiers.

## 2. Navigation
The dashboard is divided into five main sections, accessible via the sidebar on the left:

| Page Name | Purpose |
|---|---|
| Overview | High-level summary of business health and dataset scope. |
| Revenue Analysis | Deep dive into financial streams (Tickets, Merch, Drinks). |
| Customer Analysis | Demographic and geographic breakdown of the audience. |
| Experience Analysis | Evaluation of customer satisfaction and loyalty drivers. |
| Time-Based Analysis | Identification of seasonal trends and temporal patterns. |

To switch pages, simply click on the page name in the sidebar.

## 3. Using Global Filters
The sidebar contains several filters that allow you to slice the data. Filters applied on one page will persist as you navigate to other pages.

| Filter | Description |
|---|---|
| Date Range | Select a start and end date to analyze a specific time period. |
| Country | Filter data for one or more specific countries. |
| Seating Region | Focus on specific tiers like VIP, Premium, or Economy. |
| Gender | Analyze behavior by Male or Female segments. |
| Age Group | Slice data by specific age brackets (e.g., 18-25, 36-45). |
| Visit Type | Distinguish between Repeat and First-time visitors. |

Click the "Clear all filters" button at the top of the sidebar (if available) to reset all selections to their default state.

## 4. Interacting with Visualizations
All charts in this dashboard are interactive, powered by Plotly. You can perform the following actions:

| Action | How to Perform |
|---|---|
| View Details | Hover your mouse over any data point (bar, pie slice, or marker) to see a tooltip with exact values. |
| Isolate Data | Click on a category name in the chart legend to hide it. Click it again to show it. Double-click to show only that category. |
| Zoom In | Click and drag your mouse over a specific area of a chart to zoom in. |
| Reset View | Double-click anywhere on the chart area or click the "Reset axes" / "Autoscale" button in the top-right toolbar of the chart. |
| Export | Use the camera icon in the top-right toolbar of any chart to download it as a PNG image. |

## 5. Page Overviews for Business Questions
Each page is designed to answer specific business questions:

### Revenue Analysis
*   Which seating tier contributes the most to total revenue?
*   How does the revenue mix (tickets vs. merch vs. drinks) vary by country?
*   Which age groups are the highest spenders?

### Customer Analysis
*   What is the geographic distribution of our customer base?
*   How does spending behavior differ between repeat and first-time visitors?
*   Are there specific demographics (age/gender) that dominate our audience?

### Experience Analysis
*   Is there a correlation between high spending and high satisfaction?
*   Which countries have the highest likelihood to recommend our venue?
*   How does satisfaction vary between VIP and Economy guests?

### Time-Based Analysis
*   What was our best performing month for revenue?
*   Are there specific days of the week that see significantly more traffic?
*   How has our repeat visit rate trended over the last nine months?

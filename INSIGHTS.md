# Dashboard Insights - DataXplore 2.0

This document provides a detailed breakdown of the metrics, Key Performance Indicators (KPIs), and visualizations present in the DataXplore 2.0 dashboard for Company X.

## 1. Overview Page
The Overview page serves as the entry point, providing a high-level summary of the dataset and core business health.

### Key Performance Indicators (KPIs)
| KPI | Description |
|---|---|
| Total Revenue | Cumulative revenue from tickets, merchandise, and drinks. |
| Total Customers | Total count of unique customer records in the current selection. |
| Repeat Visit Rate | Percentage of customers who have visited the venue more than once. |
| Avg Satisfaction | Mean satisfaction score across all surveyed customers (Scale 0-10). |

### Dataset Statistics
| Metric | Detail |
|---|---|
| Total Records | 800 customer transactions. |
| Timeframe | January – September 2025 (9 Months). |
| Geographic Scope | 7 Countries. |
| Tiers | 4 Seating Regions (VIP, Premium, High Economy, Economy). |

## 2. Revenue Analysis
This page focuses on the financial performance of the venue across different segments.

### Revenue KPIs
| KPI | Description |
|---|---|
| Total Revenue | Combined revenue across all streams. |
| Ticket Revenue | Total earnings from ticket sales. |
| Merchandise Revenue | Total earnings from merchandise sales. |
| Drink Revenue | Total earnings from beverage sales. |
| Avg Spend per Customer | Total revenue divided by the total number of customers. |

### Visualizations
| Chart Name | Chart Type | Insights Provided |
|---|---|---|
| Revenue by Seating Region | Stacked Bar | Breakdown of Ticket, Merch, and Drink revenue for each seating tier. |
| Revenue by Country | Grouped Bar | Comparison of revenue streams across different geographic markets. |
| Seating Region Share by Country | 100% Stacked Bar | Proportional distribution of seating tiers within each country. |
| Revenue by Age Group | Stacked Bar | Revenue distribution across age brackets, split by visit type (Repeat vs First-time). |
| Revenue by Country & Seating Tier | Stacked Bar | Total revenue per country subdivided by seating region. |
| Revenue by Seating Tier & Gender | Grouped Bar | Comparison of spending habits between genders across seating tiers. |

## 3. Customer Analysis
This page explores demographics, geographic distribution, and individual spending behaviors.

### Customer KPIs
| KPI | Description |
|---|---|
| Total Customers | Count of unique visitors. |
| Avg Tickets / Visit | The average number of tickets purchased per customer transaction. |
| Repeat Visit Rate | Proportion of the audience that are returning customers. |
| Avg Spend | Mean total expenditure per customer. |

### Visualizations
| Chart Name | Chart Type | Insights Provided |
|---|---|---|
| Gender Distribution | Donut Chart | Proportion of Male vs. Female customers. |
| Seating Region Distribution | Donut Chart | Popularity of different seating tiers by customer volume. |
| Age Distribution by Gender | Histogram | Demographic spread and volume of customers by age and gender. |
| Geographic Distribution | Choropleth Map | Visual density of the customer base across the globe. |
| Spend Distribution by Region | Box Plot | Statistical distribution (median, quartiles, outliers) of spending per seating tier. |
| Repeat vs First-time Avg Spend | Grouped Bar | Comparison of the average value of a loyal customer versus a new one. |
| Age vs Total Spend | Scatter Plot | Correlation between customer age and total spending, sized by ticket volume. |

### High Spenders Analysis
| Metric | Description |
|---|---|
| Merchandise Spend Outliers | Count of customers with exceptionally high merchandise spending. |
| Drink Spend Outliers | Count of customers with exceptionally high drink spending. |
| Highest Total Spend | The single highest transaction value recorded, including context. |
| Top 10 High Spenders | Detailed table showing individual high-value transactions. |

## 4. Experience Analysis
This page measures customer satisfaction and the drivers of recommendation likelihood.

### Experience KPIs
| KPI | Description |
|---|---|
| Avg Satisfaction | Overall mean satisfaction score (0-10). |
| Avg Recommendation | Mean likelihood of a customer recommending the venue (0-10). |
| Highly Satisfied (>=7) | Percentage of customers providing a satisfaction score of 7 or higher. |
| Likely to Recommend (>=7) | Percentage of customers providing a recommendation score of 7 or higher. |

### Visualizations
| Chart Name | Chart Type | Insights Provided |
|---|---|---|
| Satisfaction by Region & Visit Type | Grouped Bar | Satisfaction levels compared across seating tiers and loyalty status. |
| Recommendation by Country | Grouped Bar | Likelihood to recommend compared across geographic markets. |
| Spend vs Satisfaction Score | Scatter + Trendline | Analyzes if higher spending leads to higher satisfaction. |
| Spend vs Recommendation Likelihood | Scatter + Trendline | Analyzes the relationship between expenditure and brand advocacy. |
| Correlation Matrix | Heatmap | Statistical relationship between all numeric variables (Age, Spend, Scores). |
| Satisfaction Distribution | Violin Plot | Visualizes the density and spread of satisfaction scores within seating tiers. |
| Country Comparison Radar | Radar Chart | Multi-dimensional comparison of Satisfaction, Recommendation, and Spend by country. |

## 5. Time-Based Analysis
This page identifies temporal patterns, seasonal trends, and growth metrics.

### Time-Based KPIs
| KPI | Description |
|---|---|
| Best Revenue Month | The month with the highest total recorded revenue. |
| Worst Revenue Month | The month with the lowest total recorded revenue. |
| MoM Revenue Growth | Month-over-Month percentage change in revenue. |
| Peak Day of Week | The day of the week with the highest volume of visits. |

### Visualizations
| Chart Name | Chart Type | Insights Provided |
|---|---|---|
| Monthly Revenue Trend | Area Chart | Overall revenue trajectory over the 9-month period. |
| Monthly Revenue by Stream | Stacked Area | Growth and contribution of Tickets, Merch, and Drinks over time. |
| Monthly Avg Satisfaction Trend | Line Chart | Tracking how customer experience changes month-to-month. |
| Visit Heatmap | Heatmap | Density of visits by Day of Week vs. Month to identify peak periods. |
| Monthly Repeat Visit Rate | Bar Chart | Tracking customer loyalty trends over time. |
| Monthly Customers by Region | Grouped Bar | Volume of customers in each seating tier across the months. |
